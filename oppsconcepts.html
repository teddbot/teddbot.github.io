Challenges and Tips

One crucial aspect of working with AWS ML is defining the problem and constructing a meaningful dataset. Understanding what to classify or predict and collecting relevant data without assumptions are key challenges. Overfitting, the risk of creating a model too specific to the dataset, necessitates careful evaluation and dataset splitting.
